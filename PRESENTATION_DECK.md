## üìä PRESENTATION TIPS

### Delivery
- **Pace**: Slow down. Pause for emphasis.
- **Energy**: Be enthusiastic but authentic
- **Eye Contact**: Look at judges, not slides
- **Gestures**: Use hands to emphasize points
- **Voice**: Vary tone, don't monotone

### Timing
- **Total**: 5 minutes (strict)
- **Problem**: 30 seconds
- **Solution**: 45 seconds
- **Demo**: 4 minutes (the hero)
- **Closing**: 30 seconds

### Demo Tips
- **Practice**: Run through 10+ times
- **Backup**: Have a video ready
- **Offline**: Don't rely on internet
- **Confidence**: Know every click
- **Wow Moments**: Pause and let it sink in

### Q&A Preparation
- **Expect**: "How is this different from X?"
- **Answer**: "We focus on teacher empowerment, not replacement"
- **Expect**: "What about privacy?"
- **Answer**: "All data stays local, no cloud required"
- **Expect**: "How do you make money?"
- **Answer**: "Freemium model, $50/month for teachers"
- **Expect**: "What's your timeline?"
- **Answer**: "MVP ready now, enterprise in 3 months"

### Slide Transitions
- Use fade transitions (0.4s)
- No distracting animations
- Let content speak for itself

### Audience Engagement
- Start with a question: "How many of you are teachers?"
- Use stories: "One teacher told us..."
- Show emotion: "This matters because..."
- End with inspiration: "Together, we can..."
